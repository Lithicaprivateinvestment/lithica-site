How to Buy BTC in the USA using CashApp, Coinbase, and Venmo

1. Using CashApp

  1. Download and open CashApp on your phone.
  2. Tap the Bitcoin icon (₿) at the bottom menu.
  3. Verify your identity if prompted.
  4. Tap “Buy Bitcoin” and enter the amount.
  5. Confirm the purchase. BTC will be added to your wallet.
  6. Tap “Withdraw Bitcoin” and paste the LITHICA BTC wallet address.

2. Using Coinbase

  1. Go to coinbase.com or use the mobile app.
  2. Create an account and verify your identity.
  3. Add a payment method (bank or debit card).
  4. Buy Bitcoin from the “Buy/Sell” menu.
  5. Navigate to “Send” and enter the LITHICA BTC wallet address.

3. Using Venmo

  1. Open the Venmo app and tap “Crypto” from the menu.
  2. Select Bitcoin (BTC).
  3. Tap “Buy” and choose the amount.
  4. Complete your purchase.
  5. Note: As of now, Venmo allows holding BTC but may restrict external transfers. Please confirm before proceeding.
